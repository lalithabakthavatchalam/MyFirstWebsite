Welcome to Dr.Tarek Surgery

Committed towards Excellent Health Care

Welcome to Dr. Tarek's Surgery. We are located at 44, Malahide Road, Artane, Dublin 5. Our Surgery provides the full range of General Practice services and committed to give the best possible GP services to all our patients.

We welcome all new patients to our practice whether they are private patients or medical card holders.Please browse our website to view our full range of services, and find out more about us. While we are dedicated to helping clients recovery we also have a passion for helping our clients lead healthy and balanced lives.

Walk-In Surgery Clinic - No Appointment Needed.

No need to book an appointment. You can walk in on your convenient time. But make sure of the opening hours of the Surgery.

Opening Hours

Monday to Friday: 8am to 1pm and 5pm to 8pm.
Saturdays appointments are available upon request.
